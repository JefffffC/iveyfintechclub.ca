This is Ivey FinTech.

FinTech and digital disruptions are reshaping the world. Ivey FinTech aims to educate and increase student exposure to digital disruption at Ivey and beyond, and to drive thought leadership on the future of financial services innovation.

The Ivey FinTech Club is providing pro-bono consultancy services for companies that are growing or looking to launch new Fintech products. We offer a fresh perspective to help organizations tackle a wide range of organizational challenges. We provide organizations with the opportunity to work with multi-faceted, ambitious and gifted students across a broad set of academic disciplines.

· · ·

Meet our business partners.

We have cultivated relationships with some of the most respected organizations in the intersections of technology and finance. The Ivey FinTech Club benefits from the support and leadership of the Scotiabank Digital Banking Lab at Ivey. Past consulting engagements have included start-ups in retail banking, insurance, artificial intelligence and more.
